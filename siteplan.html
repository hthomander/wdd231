Legal Prep Toolkit - Site Plan

Site Name

Legal Prep Toolkit

This name reflects the site's purpose as a comprehensive resource for LSAT preperation, offering study tools, tips and tracking features.

Site Purpose

The website provides LSAT study resouces, daily practice questions, a progress tracker, and law school application guidence to help pre-law students prepare efficiently.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find daily LSAT practice questions?
  • How can I track my LSAT study progress over time?

Color Schema

  • #003366 (Deep Blue):Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • #F5F5F5 (Light Gray):Used as the background color for a clean, minimalistic look.

Typography

  • 'Lora' (Serif):Used for body text for readability.
  • 'Poppins' (Sans-serif):Used for headings and navigation for a modern look.
